Allergen Policy

At ValdeBurger, we care about the health and well-being of our customers. We understand the importance of informing about the allergens present in our products. Although we do our best to avoid cross-contamination, we want our customers to be informed about the following allergens that may be present in our food:

  1. Gluten: Many of our products contain gluten due to the presence of bread and other wheat-derived ingredients. For our customers with gluten intolerance, we offer gluten-free options on our menu.
  2. Dairy: Some of our sauces and dressings may contain dairy. Please consult with our staff if you require dairy-free options.
  3. Egg: Our products may contain egg in the form of sauces, dressings or as an ingredient in bread dough. If you are allergic to egg, please let us know so that we can offer you suitable alternatives.
  4. Soy: Some of our products may contain soy in the form of additives or vegetable oils. Please consult with our staff if you require soy-free options.
  5. Nuts: Although we do not use nuts directly in our products, it is possible that some ingredients processed in our kitchen contain traces of nuts. If you are allergic to nuts, please let us know so that we can take the necessary precautions.
  6. Seafood: We do not use seafood in our core products, but it is important to note that cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. If you have a shellfish allergy, please inform our staff.

Our team is trained to answer questions about allergens and to help our customers make informed decisions about their food. If you have any questions or concerns about allergens, please do not hesitate to contact us. Your health and safety is our top priority.

Thank you for trusting ValdeBurger for your dining experience!
