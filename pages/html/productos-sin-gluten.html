Gluten-free products

At ValdeBurger, we strive to offer delicious options for all our customers, including those who follow a gluten-free diet. Here are some of our gluten-free products that you can enjoy without worry:

Classic Gluten Free Burger

Our delicious classic gluten-free burger, served with lettuce, tomato, onion and your choice of gluten-free toppings.

Gluten Free Potato Chips

Our crispy gluten free crisps, seasoned to perfection and served with your favourite dip, all gluten free.

Gluten Free Vanilla Milkshake

Refreshing and delicious gluten-free vanilla milkshake, made with high quality ingredients and no added gluten.

Gluten Free Chicken Salad

Our fresh gluten-free chicken salad with fresh vegetables and gluten-free dressing, perfect as a light and healthy option.

Gluten-free soft drink

Refreshing gluten-free drink, available in a variety of flavours to accompany your meal without worry.
